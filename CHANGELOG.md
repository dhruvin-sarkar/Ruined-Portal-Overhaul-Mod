- Replaces overworld ruined portals with large corrupted portal dungeons, three structure variants, underground cave networks, special rooms, loot caches, lava features, and portal-zone ambience.
- Adds the red storm atmosphere with sky, fog, rain, thunder, particles, rumble, and underground-friendly horizontal-distance logic.
- Adds a five-wave Gold Tribute Raid with GeckoLib Piglin Illager mobs, boss bar pacing, persistent restart recovery, and multiplayer cleanup.
- Adds post-raid rewards: lit portal, boss chest, Exiled Piglin Trader, Portal Shard locator, Nether Conduit, Ghast Tear Necklace, Corrupted Netherite armor, Nether Tide music disc, and Nether Dragon Scale trophy.
- Adds the Nether Crystal ritual and Nether Dragon boss fight with portal-centered flight targeting, End Crystal suppression, phase two, non-griefing slam, boss bar, delayed death rewards, and advancement grants for nearby players.
- Adds optional REI and Patchouli discovery content, config support through Cloth Config/ModMenu, Terralith biome exclusions, and English-valued locale stubs for seven major locales.
- Adds operator-only `/rpo` admin commands for portal locate/status/reset/wave/complete/dragon testing.
- Verified static resource shape, language keys, sound subtitles, loot data, advancement triggers, GeckoLib assets, dedicated-server dry start, client startup smoke, and Java 21 Gradle build.
